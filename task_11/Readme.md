# 3D Bazooka & Cans Game

A simple 3D browser game built with **Three.js**, where the player controls a camera and a bazooka, shooting at a stack of cans in a room.  

---

## Demo

- Shoot cans and watch realistic physics in action (gravity, bounces, collisions).  
- The bazooka has a reload animation and explosive projectiles.  
- First-person camera control.

---

## Technologies

- [Three.js](https://threejs.org/) – 3D engine  
- [Tween.js](https://github.com/tweenjs/tween.js/) – animations  
- JavaScript + HTML5 Canvas

---

## Controls

- **W/A/S/D** – move  
- **Mouse** – look around  
- **Left Click** – shoot bazooka  
- **Click on screen** – enable Pointer Lock (FPS mode)  

---

## Features

- Physically realistic cans:
  - Gravity and bounces off floor and walls  
  - Can-to-can collisions
- Bazooka:
  - Reload animation
  - Shots apply impulse to cans
- Explosion effects on impact
